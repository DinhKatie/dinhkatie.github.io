Christmas Spirits -- ACM Studio Snow Jam

This was a 2-week project to build a video game with the theme "On Ice." I was a programmer in a team of 6, and we chose a Christmas bar game as a play on words! I was responsible for the core filling drinks mechanic, general UI, and importing the writers' and artists' work. This was super fun, and although we did not include everything we wanted to, I got more practice with Unity, C#, and problem solving.

Axolotl Luncheonette -- ACM Studio Game Jam

This was a 3-day project and my first ever game jam. The theme was "You have only one __," and my team of 4 decided on a restaurant-type food serving game. I implemented the customer spawning and order processing as well as general UI. I learned a lot about Unity, C#, and keeping designs in scope in such a short timeframe.

This was a solo personal board game project based on concepts from a friend! The goal is to protect your Hero using your allies and fun card effects whilst trying to attack the enemy Hero. It is a playable two-player game made in Unity, C#, and PhotonNetwork.
